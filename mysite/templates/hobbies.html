  • Hitting the Gym
    • The gym is something I can't seem to live without. I need to hit that heavy squat or deadlift every once in a while. By the nature of my workouts, I fall into the category of Powerlifters, a group of people who train theor body moreso for overall strength gains as opposed to aesthetics. We can always diet and go on a cut later on once we hit PR numbers, but how does one really stand out at the gym? Be 145 pounds and squat more than 2x your bodyweight is how. There is a common saying in powerlifting and that is: "You're not strong unless you can bench 2x your bodyweight, squat 3x your bodyweight, and deadligt 4x your bodyweight. Alternative you can also throw in an overhead press in that statement: "Overhead press 1x your bodyweight" I have yet to hit my PR goals and may take me a few years. Injuries have set me back before but that wont stop me.
